CF SHOP! card

A discount for shopping, dining, and entertainment destinations ONLY in CF malls across Canada. The CF SHOP! card can hold anywhere from $5 to $500 - the amount you want to give is up to you. For a list of all the stores in Eaton Centre, see the link.

Purchase At Guest Services

To pick up a CF SHOP! card in person, head over to our guest services where we would be happy to help. For more information, or to buy cards in bulk, give guest services a call at 416-598-8560.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I use the card?
  • What if I want to spend more than my card limit?
  • What if I want to return the purchase I made using my card?

Using the card is easy and convenient. This card can give you more than 95% off discount of Cadillac Fairview retailers. When you arrive at a retail location, let the sales associate know you will be using the CF SHOP! card to pay for your purchase.

If your purchase costs more than the card balance, you will need to pay the remaining cost using another form of payment, such as cash. Some stores cannot split purchases between the CF SHOP! card and credit or debit cards. To check your card balance at any time, visit CFregister or call 1-800-755-8608. During regular business hours, you can visit any CF mall Guest Services. The balance is also printed on the bottom of your last receipt.

If you need to return the item you purchased with your CF SHOP! card, you will need to check with the store's return policy. Their policies may be to return the money back to your card, give you in-store credit or give you cash back. For more details or assistance contact customer service at 1-866-229-2224.
